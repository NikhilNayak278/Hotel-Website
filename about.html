About NN Residency

Located in the heart of the city, Nikhil Navaneeth Residency is a family-run boutique hotel offering comfortable rooms, warm hospitality, and modern amenities. Since 2010, we have welcomed travelers seeking a convenient location, clean rooms, and personalized service.

All rooms include free Wi‑Fi, air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and en-suite bathrooms. Guests enjoy a complimentary continental breakfast each morning and 24-hour reception for assistance with travel plans and local recommendations.
